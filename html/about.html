Looking for my resume?

Thanks for taking the time to look through my work! My name is pronounced nuh-RIN (rhymes with Corinne), and I currently work in MST. My background is designing on consulting teams, so I am a jack of all trades. I’ve designed in-person and online workshops for groups of ten, print collateral for 3000+ people events, and physical installations across different sectors and varying company sizes. I’ve also done the odd virtual reality environment, made a few images in Processing, and modeled toy parts in Illustrator—whatever the job has called for.

I don't believe in one-person shows. Every large, complicated event I've designed for was made by teams of high-caliber colleagues working through ambiguity and remaining flexible through inevitable and unpredictable pitfalls. I am forever grateful for my teammates for sharing their knowledge and kindness with me.

Outside of design, I sew, weave, draw, write short essays, write waltzes, and am a recovering serial hobbyist. You can see my creative endeavors at curio_amare on Instagram or visit Joeseph's Coat to see me work on weavings.
